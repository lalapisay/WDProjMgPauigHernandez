About Games

The atom merging game is a way to learn chemistry nomenclature, where merging atoms lead to different names. The balancing equations game is a way to test and practice balancing chemical equations. The hybridization model is a way to understand how hybridization in atoms work. The quizzes are used to test your understanding of the common chemistry topics and principles.

Why was this made?

We made this to help the incoming students that will be taking chemistry 1. This aims to provide an interactive and engaging learning experience to reinforce fundamental concepts in chemistry. We provided fun games and quizzes to enhance their understanding of chemistry without it becoming boring.
